FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to have my CV ready before ordering? +
No. You fill in our detailed intake questionnaire after payment. We write everything from scratch based on your answers. If you have an existing CV, you can share it too — but it's not required.
What's the difference between an English CV and a German Lebenslauf? +
German Lebenslauf format follows specific conventions — reverse chronological structure, optional photo, formal tone, different section ordering, and specific expectations around personal information. We know exactly what German hiring managers expect and write accordingly.
How does the delivery process work? +
The process starts when you submit your completed intake form, not when you pay. Once we receive your answers, your rewrite is delivered to your Google Drive as soon as it is ready. You will be notified by email the moment your files are available.
What if I'm not happy with the result? +
Every package includes at least one revision round. Tell us exactly what you'd like changed and we'll update it within 24 hours. We want you walking into interviews, not asking for refunds.
Do you only work with engineers? +
We specialise in mechanical, industrial, and automotive engineering roles in the German market — that's our deepest expertise. We also work with data professionals, manufacturing, and technical consulting roles. If you're unsure, send us a message before ordering.
